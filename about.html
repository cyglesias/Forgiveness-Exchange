What is the Forgiveness Exchange?

The Forgiveness Exchange is a reciprocal platform that facilitates connection through offering and asking for forgiveness, anonymously.

How does it work?

First, you'll read an anonymous submission by another user and respond with an offer of forgiveness. Think about how your words can encourage healing and to the person you are writing to. Then, you can submit your own request for forgiveness. Write about a regret, something you wish you could take back, a secret, anything for which you wish you could be forgiven. Later, another anonymous user will respond to your note, which you can view at a private link.  .

What are the ground rules?

The Forgiveness Exchange is all about connection, kindness, openness, and empathy, so please write with these values in mind. Hateful submissions will be deleted.

Who are we?

The Forgiveness Exchange is a project by artist Christina Yglesias.
